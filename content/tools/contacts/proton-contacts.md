Proton Contacts helps keep address book details inside the Proton ecosystem. Key features include:

- Encrypted contact detail fields
- Integration with Proton Mail
- Contact groups for organizing people and lists
- Import tools for moving contacts from other providers
- Good fit for people already using Proton Mail and Calendar
