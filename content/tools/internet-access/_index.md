Explore internet access options where the provider itself is part of the privacy threat model.

These services are not anonymity tools. They can still see account, billing, service-location, network, and legal-request surfaces. Treat them as practical connectivity choices, then layer DNS, VPN, Tor, router, and device hardening based on your threat model.
