Cape is a privacy-focused US mobile carrier that builds privacy and security controls into cellular service. Key features include:

- Minimal-data account posture compared with mainstream carriers
- Identifier Rotation on supported iPhone and Pixel devices
- SIM-swap protection tied to a user-held recovery phrase
- Secondary numbers for compartmentalizing SMS use
- Encrypted voicemail and last-mile encrypted texting features

Use Cape when you want a more private managed US phone plan and are willing to pay for carrier-level privacy features. Cellular service still involves network coverage, supported-device limits, device identifiers, and lawful-request exposure.
