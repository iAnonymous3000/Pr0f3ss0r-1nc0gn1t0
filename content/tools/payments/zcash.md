Zcash is a privacy-preserving cryptocurrency with some of the strongest cryptographic privacy technology ever deployed in a live digital payments system. Key features include:
- Industry-leading shielded transactions
- Zero-knowledge proofs for strong on-chain privacy
- Selective disclosure when users need it
- Deep scientific and cryptographic research foundation
- Ongoing audits, protocol work, and serious engineering rigor
