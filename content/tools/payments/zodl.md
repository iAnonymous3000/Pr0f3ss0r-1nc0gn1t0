ZODL is a mobile wallet built to make Zcash shielded payments practical in everyday use. Key features include:
- Mobile-first Zcash wallet experience
- Designed around private shielded transactions
- Straightforward send and receive flows
- Cleaner everyday usability for privacy-conscious users
- Built by the Zcash Open Development Lab
