Cash remains one of the strongest privacy-preserving payment methods for in-person transactions. Key features include:

- No account required
- No app, device, or network dependency
- Minimal metadata when used directly
- Works during outages and account lockouts
- Useful for small everyday purchases where accepted

Cash is not perfect for every threat model, but it is still an important privacy baseline for local payments.
