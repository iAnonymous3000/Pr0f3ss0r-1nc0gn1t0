An RFID passport sleeve is a small add-on for people who travel with an e-passport and want an extra layer of shielding while the passport is stored. Many e-passports already include anti-skimming protections, so this is a belt-and-suspenders item rather than a general requirement.

What to look for:

- A sleeve that fully covers the closed passport
- Durable material that does not tear during repeated airport use
- A slim profile that still fits your normal travel document setup
- Easy removal so border and airport processes are not slowed down unnecessarily
- A real need for the extra layer, especially if your passport is already kept closed inside a protected travel document holder
