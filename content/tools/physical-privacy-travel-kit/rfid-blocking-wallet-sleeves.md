RFID-blocking wallets and sleeves can be useful for access cards, transit cards, enhanced IDs, and other cards that are always readable at short range. For modern contactless payment cards, the fraud risk from casual skimming is usually limited by short read distance, transaction controls, and dynamic payment data, so this is not an essential baseline item for everyone.

What to look for:

- A design that fully covers the cards you carry
- A form factor that fits your daily habits
- Reliable blocking for the specific card types you actually use
- A simple reader test to confirm the relevant card does not read while inside the sleeve or wallet
- Enough usability that you still remove the intended card cleanly instead of tapping a whole wallet full of cards
