A focused travel kit for protecting digital devices, digital identity, payment cards, screens, charging paths, and wireless exposure when working away from trusted spaces.

If you only buy one thing from this kit, start with a trusted wall charger and cables; if you regularly run out of power while traveling, add a reputable power bank before worrying about RFID or Faraday gear.

Label standard:

- Essential: broadly useful, low-regret controls for common public-space and travel-device risks.
- Situational: valid for specific documents, cards, devices, destinations, or threat models, but not necessary for everyone.
