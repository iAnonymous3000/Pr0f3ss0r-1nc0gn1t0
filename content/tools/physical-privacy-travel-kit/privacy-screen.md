A privacy screen helps reduce shoulder-surfing when you use a laptop, tablet, or phone in public spaces, shared offices, airports, trains, cafes, or classrooms.

What to look for:

- Exact fit for the device size and aspect ratio
- Removable or magnetic mounting if you care about color accuracy
- A viewing angle that meaningfully obscures side glances
- Acceptable brightness loss for your normal work environment
- A surface that can be cleaned without making text harder to read
