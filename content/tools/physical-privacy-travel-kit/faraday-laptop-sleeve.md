A Faraday laptop sleeve is a niche option for people who need temporary wireless isolation for a laptop during travel, storage, or controlled handoff situations.

What to look for:

- A sleeve that fits the laptop without stressing the closure
- Clear shielding claims for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular, GPS, and NFC frequencies
- A closure that remains sealed in a bag or backpack
- A practical reason to carry it, since it adds bulk and blocks legitimate connectivity while in use
