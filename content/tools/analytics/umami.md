Umami is an open-source analytics platform for people who want a lightweight Google Analytics alternative that can be self-hosted. Key features include:

- Privacy-focused analytics
- Simple dashboard for traffic and referrers
- Self-hosted and cloud options
- Event tracking for custom site actions
- Good fit for technical site owners who want a small, understandable analytics stack
