Simple Analytics is a privacy-focused analytics service for people who want understandable site metrics without behavioral ad tracking. Key features include:

- Cookie-free analytics
- Clean traffic, referrer, and page-performance reporting
- Event and goal tracking for site owners who need more detail
- Built for easier privacy compliance
- Good fit for creators and small teams that want a hosted analytics product
