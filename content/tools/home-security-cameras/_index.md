Explore home security camera systems that keep footage local where possible and make retention, remote access, licensing, and household privacy tradeoffs explicit.

Cameras are unusually sensitive devices: they can capture private rooms, guests, neighbors, routines, and physical security events. Prefer wired cameras, local recording, narrow fields of view, clear retention limits, and camera placement that solves a real safety problem without turning the whole home into a recording zone.
