qBittorrent is a free and open-source BitTorrent client that aims to provide a clean alternative to ad-supported torrent clients. Key features include:

- No ads or bundled junkware
- Cross-platform desktop support
- Built-in search support through plugins
- Web interface for remote management
- Good fit for legitimate peer-to-peer downloads and open-source distribution workflows
