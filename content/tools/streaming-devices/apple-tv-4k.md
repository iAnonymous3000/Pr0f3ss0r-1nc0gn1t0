Apple TV 4K is the clearest mainstream streaming-device pick if you want better privacy defaults instead of an ecosystem built around tracking what you watch. Key features include:
- No ACR-based viewing-data business model
- Opt-out analytics instead of mandatory ad-tech profiling
- No ad-supported OS layer built around monetizing viewing habits
- Tight integration with the Apple ecosystem, including the Home app and smart home hub support
- Strong fit for people who want a mainstream streaming box that does not sell their viewing data as the product
