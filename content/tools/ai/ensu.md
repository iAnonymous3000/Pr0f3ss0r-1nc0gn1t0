Ensu is Ente's private, local-first AI chat app that runs inference directly on your device. Key features include:
- On-device inference with no cloud dependency for chats
- Works offline once the model is downloaded
- No account, no tracking, no usage limits, and no cost
- Open source and available across major desktop and mobile platforms
- Supports image attachments for multimodal conversations on supported platforms
