Kagi Search is a paid search engine designed around ad-free results and user control. Key features include:

- No advertising-based ranking incentives
- No behavioral ad tracking
- Customizable result ranking
- Lenses for focused search contexts
- Fast access to related Kagi tools such as Summarizer and Translate

Kagi is a strong option for users who would rather pay directly for search than pay indirectly through attention and profiling.
