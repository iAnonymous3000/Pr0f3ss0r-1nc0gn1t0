OsmAnd is an offline map and navigation app built around OpenStreetMap with far more depth than most mainstream options. Key features include:
- Offline maps and turn-by-turn navigation across multiple travel modes
- Extensive route settings, layers, and topographic detail
- Support for GPX tracks and advanced trip planning
- Broad customization for serious travel, hiking, and cycling use
- Better fit for power users who want depth over polish
