Apple Maps is Apple's mainstream mapping and navigation app across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, CarPlay, and the web. Key features include:
- Deep integration across Apple devices and services
- Driving, walking, cycling, and transit directions in supported regions
- Guides, place cards, and ETA sharing
- Look Around imagery in supported locations
- Strong fit for Apple users who want a mainstream maps app inside the Apple ecosystem
