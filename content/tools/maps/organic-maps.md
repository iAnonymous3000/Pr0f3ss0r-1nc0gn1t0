Organic Maps is an open-source, offline-first navigation app based on OpenStreetMap data. Key features include:
- Worldwide offline maps for walking, driving, and cycling
- No ads, no tracking, and no account requirement
- Strong hiking, walking, and cycling route coverage
- Turn-by-turn navigation with voice guidance
- Great fit for people who want a simple, privacy-respecting maps app
