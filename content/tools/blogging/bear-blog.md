Bear Blog is a lightweight blogging platform focused on simple publishing instead of heavy themes, scripts, and tracking. Key features include:

- No built-in trackers or ads
- Fast, minimal pages
- Custom domain support
- RSS and Atom feeds
- Good fit for personal blogs, essays, and low-maintenance publishing
