ONLYOFFICE is an office suite for documents, spreadsheets, presentations, forms, and PDFs. Key features include:

- Desktop, mobile, cloud, and self-hosted options
- Strong compatibility with Microsoft Office formats
- Collaborative editing for teams
- Open-source community editions
- Good fit for people who need familiar office workflows with more deployment flexibility
