Proton Docs & Sheets brings private online documents and spreadsheets into the Proton ecosystem. Key features include:
- End-to-end encrypted documents and spreadsheets
- Real-time collaboration and commenting
- Secure sharing with internal and external collaborators
- Part of the Proton Drive workspace for file storage and access across devices
- Strong fit for people who want Google Docs-style collaboration without giving up privacy
