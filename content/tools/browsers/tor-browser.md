Tor Browser is designed for users who need stronger anonymity and resistance to tracking online. Key features include:
- Routes traffic over the Tor network
- Strong anti-fingerprinting protections
- Isolation features that reduce cross-site tracking
- Hardened defaults for privacy-sensitive browsing
- Widely used tool for anonymity and censorship resistance
