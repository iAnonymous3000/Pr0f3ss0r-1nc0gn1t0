Mullvad Browser is a privacy-focused browser designed to reduce tracking and fingerprinting. Key features include:
- Strong anti-fingerprinting defaults
- Collaboration with the Tor Browser project
- Browser privacy hardening out of the box
- Reduced need for extension-heavy setups
- Good fit for users who want strong privacy without the Tor network itself
